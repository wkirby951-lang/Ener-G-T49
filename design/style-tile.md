## Typography

### Headings: Montserrat
- H1: Bold 48px → 32px mobile
- H2: Bold 36px → 26px mobile  
- H3: Semi-Bold 28px → 22px mobile
- H4: Semi-Bold 22px → 18px mobile
- Weight: 700 (Bold), 600 (Semi-Bold), 500 (Medium)

### Body: Inter
- Body: Regular 16px → 15px mobile
- Small: Regular 13px
- Caption: Regular 12px
- Weight: 400 (Regular), 500 (Medium), 600 (Semi-Bold for small emphasis)

- Buttons: 20px pill
- Cards: 12px

## Shadows
- Card shadow: `0 2px 8px rgba(26, 42, 58, 0.08)`
- Elevated card: `0 4px 16px rgba(26, 42, 58, 0.12)`
- Modal overlay: `0 8px 32px rgba(26, 42, 58, 0.2)`

## Gradients
- Hero gradient: `linear-gradient(135deg, #1A2A3A 0%, #2C6E9C 100%)`
- CTA gradient: `linear-gradient(135deg, #2C6E9C 0%, #4A9E8E 100%)`
- Accent glow: `radial-gradient(circle, rgba(74, 158, 142, 0.15) 0%, transparent 70%)`

## Logo Usage
- **Minimum clear space:** 16px around logo
- **Minimum size:** 32px (icon only), 120px (with wordmark)
- **Dark background:** Use white/light version or full-color logo (works on navy)
- **Light background:** Use full-color logo on sand/ivory

## Age Segment Colors (for callout cards)
- Teens (14–18): Teal accent `#4A9E8E` — vibrant, energetic, approachable
- Young Adults (19–36): Ocean blue `#2C6E9C` — professional, balanced
- Adults (37–65+): Sage green `#7CA982` — mature, calming, trustworthy

## Tone in Design
- **Calming** — generous white space, soft curves, muted gradients
- **Professional** — structured grids, consistent alignment, clear hierarchy
- **Welcoming** — warm neutrals, friendly rounded corners, approachable iconography
- **Science-backed** — clean typography, data visualizations (progress rings, streaks), structured layouts
