# Silva Mind Control Guided Session — Adults (37–65+)

**Duration:** 25 minutes  
**Age Group:** Adults (37–65+)  
**Focus:** Life transitions, deep relaxation, intuitive guidance, inner wisdom, sleep enhancement  
**Goal:** Enter alpha state, meet your "inner counselor," explore a life question

---

## Script

### Phase 1: Deep Countdown to Alpha (0:00–5:00)

*(Deep, unhurried, resonant voice — each pause is generous)*

"Welcome. I invite you to settle into a comfortable position. Let your spine be straight but not rigid. Let your hands rest wherever they naturally fall."

*(Pause 5 seconds)*

"Take a long, slow breath. In through your nose… and out through your mouth, releasing everything you're carrying."

*(Pause 6 seconds)*

"One more — deeper this time. Inhale fully… hold for a moment… and exhale completely, letting go of tension, worry, and time."

*(Pause 7 seconds)*

"We'll now descend into the alpha level — a state of deep relaxation where your mind is open, receptive, and connected to its deepest wisdom. I'll count from 10 to 1. Each number will take you deeper."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"10… Relax your feet. Feel them become warm and heavy. All the tension in your feet — let it drain away."

*(Pause 6 seconds)*

"9… Relax your legs — calves, knees, thighs. Let them sink into the surface beneath you."

*(Pause 6 seconds)*

"8… Relax your hips and lower back. This is where many of us store years of tension. Let it begin to soften now."

*(Pause 7 seconds)*

"7… Relax your abdomen and stomach. Soft belly. Gentle, rhythmic breathing."

*(Pause 6 seconds)*

"6… Relax your chest and upper back. Let your breath be full and easy. Expand with each inhale."

*(Pause 6 seconds)*

"5… Relax your shoulders. You've been carrying so much. Let them drop now. Let them be free."

*(Pause 6 seconds)*

"4… Relax your arms and hands. Heavy, warm, completely at rest."

*(Pause 6 seconds)*

"3… Relax your neck, jaw, and face. Unclench your jaw. Soften your forehead. Let your eyes rest deeply in their sockets."

*(Pause 7 seconds)*

"2… Your entire body is deeply relaxed. Warm. Heavy. Peaceful. Your mind is becoming quiet. Thoughts float by like clouds, and you let them pass."

*(Pause 7 seconds)*

"1… You are now at the alpha level. A state of deep, aware relaxation. Your conscious mind is quiet. Your deeper mind is open. You are in the space between thoughts — the place of intuition, creativity, and healing."

*(Pause 8 seconds)*

---

### Phase 2: Meeting Your Inner Counselor (5:00–12:00)

"From this alpha level, imagine yourself walking down a beautiful path. Notice the details around you — is it a forest path, a beach, a garden, a mountain trail?"

*(Pause 8 seconds)*

"Notice the air — is it warm or cool? The light — is it bright or soft? The sounds around you — birds, water, wind, silence."

*(Pause 8 seconds)*

"As you walk, you begin to notice a gentle glow ahead. As you approach, you see that the glow is coming from a wise presence — your inner counselor. This is the part of you that knows more than your conscious mind knows. It may appear as a wise elder, a guide, a light, or simply a knowing presence."

*(Pause 10 seconds)*

"Approach this presence with respect and openness. You can sit or stand before them."

*(Pause 6 seconds)*

"Now, silently or aloud, ask your inner counselor a question. It could be about a life transition you're navigating, a decision you're facing, a pattern you're trying to understand, or simply: 'What do I most need to know right now?'"

*(Pause 12 seconds)*

"Listen. The answer may not come in words. It may come as a feeling, an image, a memory, or a knowing. Trust whatever arises. It doesn't have to make sense to your logical mind."

*(Pause 12 seconds)*

"If you received a message, thank your inner counselor. If you didn't receive anything clear, that's okay — your deeper mind heard you, and the answer will unfold in the coming days."

*(Pause 6 seconds)*

"Before leaving, ask: 'What is the one thing I can do today to move toward greater peace and alignment?'"

*(Pause 10 seconds)*

"Receive whatever comes, and thank your inner counselor for their wisdom."

*(Pause 6 seconds)*

---

### Phase 3: The Glass of Water Technique (12:00–15:00)

*(Gentle transition)*

"I'll now guide you through a classic Silva technique you can use on your own."

*(Pause 3 seconds)*

"Imagine a glass of pure, clear water in your hands. See the light passing through it. Feel the coolness of the glass."

*(Pause 6 seconds)*

"Program this water with a specific intention. Hold the glass and repeat silently three times:"

"'This water will help my deeper mind work on [your question or intention] while I sleep tonight.'"

*(Pause 8 seconds)*

"Drink half the glass before bed, and the other half when you wake. Your deeper mind will continue working on your intention throughout the night."

*(Pause 5 seconds)*

"Hold that intention now — see it, feel it, trust it."

*(Pause 6 seconds)*

---

### Phase 4: Deepening & Integration (15:00–20:00)

"We'll now take a few more moments at this deep level. Allow any insights from your inner counselor to settle into your consciousness."

*(Pause 6 seconds)*

"Bring to mind one specific action you can take in the next 24 hours — no matter how small — that aligns with the wisdom you received today."

*(Pause 8 seconds)*

"See yourself taking that action. Feel the satisfaction of following through."

*(Pause 6 seconds)*

"Know that your deeper mind is now working on your behalf, even as you rest. Solutions will emerge. Clarity will come."

*(Pause 6 seconds)*

"Now, bring your thumb, index, and middle finger together — your three-finger anchor. From now on, any time you touch these three fingers together, you will instantly return to this alpha state — calm, centered, and connected to your deeper wisdom."

*(Pause 6 seconds)*

"Repeat silently: 'Whenever I bring these three fingers together, I immediately enter my optimal mental and spiritual state — relaxed, focused, and receptive.'"

*(Pause 6 seconds)*

---

### Phase 5: Gentle Return to Full Awareness (20:00–25:00)

"Now we'll return gradually, bringing the peace and wisdom of alpha with us."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"1… 2… 3 — beginning to become aware of your body in the room."

*(Pause 5 seconds)*

"4… 5… 6 — gently moving your fingers and toes. Taking a fuller breath."

*(Pause 5 seconds)*

"7… 8… 9 — feeling refreshed, clear, and deeply rested."

*(Pause 5 seconds)*

"10 — when you're ready, gently open your eyes. Take your time."

*(Pause 6 seconds)*

"Welcome back. Notice how different your body feels — the quality of your breath, the stillness you're carrying."

*(Pause 5 seconds)*

"Take a moment. You may want to journal any insights, images, or messages that came through. These are gifts from your deeper mind."

*(Pause 6 seconds)*

"Before you go, touch your three fingers together and set one intention for the rest of your day — something like: 'I carry this peace with me,' or 'I trust the answers are coming.'"

*(Pause 5 seconds)*

"You've done deep, meaningful work. Thank yourself. Thank your deeper mind. And know that this wisdom is always available to you."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"Namaste."

---

**End of session.**

*The Silva Method is a self-development practice. It is not a replacement for medical or mental health treatment.*
