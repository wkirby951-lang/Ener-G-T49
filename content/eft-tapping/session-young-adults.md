# EFT Tapping Guided Session — Young Adults (19–36)

**Duration:** 16 minutes  
**Age Group:** Young Adults (19–36)  
**Focus:** Career stress, burnout, relationship anxiety, financial worry  
**Goal:** Reduce distress from a specific stressor by 3+ points using the full EFT tapping sequence

---

## Script

### Phase 1: Check-In & Setup (0:00–2:30)

*(Warm, professional, grounded voice)*

"Welcome. Take a moment to arrive. Settle into your seat. Feet flat on the floor. Hands resting in your lap."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"Take a deep breath. In through your nose — fill your lungs completely. And exhale slowly through your mouth, making a soft sigh sound."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"Now, bring to mind a specific stressor that's been weighing on you. Something that's been showing up in your thoughts — a work situation, a relationship dynamic, a financial worry, a decision you're struggling with."

*(Pause 5 seconds)*

"Describe it briefly to yourself. What's the situation? What feelings come up? What negative belief is attached?"

*(Pause 6 seconds)*

"Now rate the intensity of this stressor — 0 being no disturbance at all, 10 being the most intense you can imagine. Just get a number."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"Let's begin tapping. Locate your karate chop point — the fleshy side of your hand, between your pinky finger and your wrist."

---

### Phase 2: Setup Statement (2:30–3:30)

"Tap the karate chop point gently but continuously. We'll say the setup statement together."

*(Pace slows)*

"Even though I'm carrying this stress about [describe the issue], I deeply and completely accept myself."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"Even though I feel [anxious / overwhelmed / frustrated / scared / hurt], I accept all of my feelings right now. They're valid."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"Even though this situation feels stuck or heavy, I'm open to seeing it differently. I'm open to feeling better."

*(Pause 3 seconds)*

---

### Phase 3: The Full Tapping Sequence — Round 1 (3:30–7:00)

*(Rhythmic, steady pace — 5–7 taps per point)*

"We'll now tap through all 8 points. Repeat the reminder phrase after me, or just say it in your mind."

**Eyebrow:** *(tap)* "This stress about [the issue]…"

**Side of Eye:** *(tap)* "Feeling it in my body…"

**Under Eye:** *(tap)* "All these emotions…"

**Under Nose:** *(tap)* "This heaviness in my chest…"

**Chin:** *(tap)* "The stories I keep telling myself…"

**Collarbone:** *(tap)* "This feeling of… [not enough / stuck / alone / overwhelmed]"

**Under Arm:** *(tap)* "All this tension…"

**Top of Head:** *(tap)* "This whole situation…"

*(Pause 4 seconds — deep breath)*

---

### Phase 4: Round 2 — Acknowledgement (7:00–10:00)

"Let's go deeper. This round, we acknowledge the resistance."

**Karate Chop:** *(tap)* "Even though some part of me doesn't want to let this go, I accept that too."

**Eyebrow:** *(tap)* "I don't know if I can let this go…"

**Side of Eye:** *(tap)* "What if I'm supposed to hold onto this worry?"

**Under Eye:** *(tap)* "It feels unsafe to relax…"

**Under Nose:** *(tap)* "This stress has been protecting me…"

**Chin:** *(tap)* "I'm learning I can be safe AND relaxed…"

**Collarbone:** *(tap)* "I release the tension I don't need…"

**Under Arm:** *(tap)* "Letting go of control, just a little…"

**Top of Head:** *(tap)* "I'm safe. I'm capable. I can handle what comes."

*(Pause 5 seconds — deep breath)*

"Notice any shifts. Maybe a deep breath. A yawn. A sigh. These are signs of release."

*(Pause 3 seconds)*

---

### Phase 5: Round 3 — Reframing (10:00–13:00)

"This round, we introduce a new possibility."

**Eyebrow:** *(tap)* "Even with this challenge…"

**Side of Eye:** *(tap)* "I'm still here, still capable…"

**Under Eye:** *(tap)* "I've handled hard things before…"

**Under Nose:** *(tap)* "I can handle this too…"

**Chin:** *(tap)* "I'm doing my best with what I have…"

**Collarbone:** *(tap)* "It doesn't have to be perfect…"

**Under Arm:** *(tap)* "I give myself permission to feel better…"

**Top of Head:** *(tap)* "I choose peace. I choose clarity."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

**Karate Chop:** *(tap)* "Even though this situation hasn't fully resolved, I trust myself to navigate it. I choose to feel less burdened right now."

*(Pause 3 seconds)*

**Eyebrow:** *(tap)* "This remaining tension…"

**Side of Eye:** *(tap)* "Releasing it now…"

**Under Eye:** *(tap)* "My body deserves rest…"

**Under Nose:** *(tap)* "My mind deserves peace…"

**Chin:** *(tap)* "I am worthy of relief…"

**Collarbone:** *(tap)* "I'm not my stress…"

**Under Arm:** *(tap)* "I am more than this problem…"

**Top of Head:** *(tap)* "Clear mind. Open heart. Steady breath."

*(Pause 5 seconds — deep breath)*

---

### Phase 6: Check-In & Close (13:00–16:00)

"Take a full, deep breath. Let your hands rest."

*(Pause 5 seconds)*

"Re-rate your intensity level from 0 to 10. What number came up?"

*(Pause 5 seconds)*

"Whatever shift you experienced — even 1 or 2 points — that's real. That's your nervous system responding."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"Let's do a quick body scan starting from your head…"

*(Slowly, with pauses)*

"Jaw — is it tight? Let it soften."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"Shoulders — let them drop."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"Chest and belly — soft. Breathing."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"Hips and legs — letting gravity hold them."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"Name three things you can see in the room."

*(Pause 5 seconds)*

"Notice how your body feels compared to when we started. Even a small shift matters."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"This is a practice. Each time you tap, you're teaching your nervous system that it's safe to let go. The more you practice, the faster the shifts come."

*(Pause 3 seconds)*

"If the number is still above 5, you might benefit from another round on the same issue later, or breaking it into smaller parts to tap on one at a time."

*(Pause 3 seconds)*

"Thank you for showing up for yourself. Take another breath. And when you're ready, gently open your eyes."

---

**End of session.**

*Tip: For recurring stressors, try tapping daily at the same time to build a new stress-response pattern.*
