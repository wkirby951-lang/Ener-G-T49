# EFT Tapping Guided Session — Adults (37–65+)

**Duration:** 25 minutes  
**Age Group:** Adults (37–65+)  
**Focus:** Chronic stress, grief, life transitions, sleep issues, long-standing emotional patterns  
**Goal:** Work through deeper emotional layers using extended EFT tapping with body awareness and cognitive reframing

---

## Script

### Phase 1: Setting the Space & Centering (0:00–4:00)

*(Warm, unhurried, deeply grounded voice — allows spacious pauses)*

"Hello. Thank you for being here with yourself today. This is your time."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"Find a comfortable seated position. If you can, sit upright enough to stay alert, but relaxed enough to feel at ease. Let your hands rest wherever they naturally fall."

*(Pause 5 seconds)*

"Take three slow, deep breaths. I'll count you through them."

*(Pause 2 seconds)*

"First breath — in through your nose… 1… 2… 3… 4… and out through your mouth… 1… 2… 3… 4… 5… 6."

*(Pause 5 seconds)*

"Second breath — in… hold… and longer out."

*(Pause 6 seconds)*

"Third breath — let your belly soften. In… and all the way out."

*(Pause 5 seconds)*

"Now, bring to mind something you'd like to work with today. This could be:"

- A recurring stressor that drains your energy
- A grief that hasn't fully been processed
- A life transition — retirement, empty nest, aging, health changes
- A pattern you've noticed — people-pleasing, self-criticism, worry
- A specific memory that still carries emotional charge

*(Pause 10 seconds)*

"Let it surface naturally. Don't force it. Trust whatever shows up."

*(Pause 8 seconds)*

"Now, rate this issue's intensity from 0 to 10 — where 0 is no disturbance at all, and 10 is the most overwhelming you can imagine. Don't overthink the number. Your first instinct is usually right."

*(Pause 5 seconds)*

"We'll come back to that number later. For now, just hold it gently."

---

### Phase 2: Setup & Beginner's Mind (4:00–6:00)

"Find your karate chop point. The fleshy side of your hand."

*(Pause 2 seconds)*

"Begin tapping there, gently but steadily."

*(Pause 3 seconds)*

"Let's say the setup statement together. I'll say it first — you can repeat it aloud or silently."

"Even though I've been carrying this weight for a long time — maybe years — I choose to release some of it now. I deeply and completely accept myself."

*(Pause 5 seconds)*

"Even though this feeling is familiar — it's almost like an old friend I don't know how to live without — I'm open to letting some of it go today."

*(Pause 5 seconds)*

"Even though a part of me is scared of what might be underneath this feeling, I trust that I am ready to process whatever comes. I am safe enough to heal."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

---

### Phase 3: The Extended Tapping Sequence — Round 1 (6:00–10:00)

*(Steady, rhythmic, compassionate voice)*

"We'll tap through the points now. Say the reminder phrase or hold the feeling in your mind."

**Eyebrow:** *(tap)* "This heavy feeling I've been carrying…"

**Side of Eye:** *(tap)* "This weight in my chest / shoulders / belly…"

**Under Eye:** *(tap)* "All these years of holding on…"

**Under Nose:** *(tap)* "This familiar ache…"

**Chin:** *(tap)* "This story I've been telling myself…"

**Collarbone:** *(tap)* "This fear of letting go…"

**Under Arm:** *(tap)* "All the unshed tears…"

**Top of Head:** *(tap)* "This deep, quiet knowing that something needs to shift…"

*(Pause 5 seconds — slow breath)*

"Good. Take a breath. Notice what surfaced — an image, a word, a sensation."

**Karate Chop:** *(tap)* "Even though there's resistance, I honor it. It's been protecting me. I'm safe enough to explore."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

**Eyebrow:** *(tap)* "This resistance…"

**Side of Eye:** *(tap)* "It's okay to take my time…"

**Under Eye:** *(tap)* "I don't have to fix everything today…"

**Under Nose:** *(tap)* "Just one layer, one moment at a time…"

**Chin:** *(tap)* "I give myself permission to feel…"

**Collarbone:** *(tap)* "Permission to release…"

**Under Arm:** *(tap)* "Permission to heal at my own pace…"

**Top of Head:** *(tap)* "I am held. I am safe. I am ready."

*(Pause 6 seconds — let user breathe)*

---

### Phase 4: Round 2 — Grief & Letting Go (10:00–14:30)

"Now we'll work with what came up. If grief, sadness, or loss surfaced, this round is for you."

**Karate Chop:** *(tap)* "Even though I've been holding onto this grief, I give myself permission to grieve fully. I am not broken for feeling this way."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

**Eyebrow:** *(tap)* "This sadness that lives in my body…"

**Side of Eye:** *(tap)* "The tears I haven't shed…"

**Under Eye:** *(tap)* "The person / version of life I've lost…"

**Under Nose:** *(tap)* "This ache of things left unsaid…"

**Chin:** *(tap)* "The weight of unexpressed love…"

**Collarbone:** *(tap)* "I honor this grief. It matters."

**Under Arm:** *(tap)* "It's not too much. I'm not too much."

**Top of Head:** *(tap)* "Grief is love with nowhere to go. I choose to let love remain."

*(Pause 6 seconds)*

"Take a moment. Let whatever needs to flow, flow. There's no rush."

*(Pause 8 seconds)*

"Continue tapping wherever feels right, or follow me through the points again."

**Eyebrow:** *(tap)* "I give this grief space…"

**Side of Eye:** *(tap)* "I don't need to rush past it…"

**Under Eye:** *(tap)* "Healing happens at its own speed…"

**Under Nose:** *(tap)* "I'm learning to be tender with myself…"

**Chin:** *(tap)* "I am resilient AND I am gentle…"

**Collarbone:** *(tap)* "Both can be true…"

**Under Arm:** *(tap)* "I am still whole, even in my grief…"

**Top of Head:** *(tap)* "Peace. Deep, quiet peace."

*(Pause 6 seconds)*

---

### Phase 5: Round 3 — Integration & Reframing (14:30–19:00)

"Now we move toward integration. What have you learned from this experience? What strength did you discover you didn't know you had?"

*(Pause 6 seconds)*

"Let's tap in the new story."

**Karate Chop:** *(tap)* "Even though I've been shaped by this experience, I am not defined by it. I am more than what happened to me."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

**Eyebrow:** *(tap)* "I choose to see myself with compassion…"

**Side of Eye:** *(tap)* "I did the best I could with what I knew…"

**Under Eye:** *(tap)* "And now I know more, so I can do better…"

**Under Nose:** *(tap)* "For myself."

**Chin:** *(tap)* "I release the need to be who I was before…"

**Collarbone:** *(tap)* "I welcome who I'm becoming…"

**Under Arm:** *(tap)* "Wisdom comes through experience, not despite it…"

**Top of Head:** *(tap)* "I am not broken. I am healing."

*(Pause 5 seconds)*

**Eyebrow:** *(tap)* "This new story feels…"

**Side of Eye:** *(tap)* "A little unfamiliar, but true…"

**Under Eye:** *(tap)* "I am allowed to feel lighter…"

**Under Nose:** *(tap)* "I am allowed to feel joy again…"

**Chin:** *(tap)* "I am allowed to rest…"

**Collarbone:** *(tap)* "This is not a betrayal of the past…"

**Under Arm:** *(tap)* "This is honoring how far I've come…"

**Top of Head:** *(tap)* "I claim my peace. I claim my wholeness."

*(Pause 6 seconds)*

---

### Phase 6: Body Scan & Closure (19:00–25:00)

"Let your hands rest. Take a full, slow breath."

*(Pause 5 seconds)*

"Scan your body from the crown of your head to the soles of your feet."

"Top of your head… any sensations?"

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"Forehead, eyes, jaw — soft?"

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"Neck and shoulders — letting go?"

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"Chest and heart — what do you feel here now?"

*(Pause 5 seconds)*

"Belly — full and soft."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"Hips — the seat of your resilience."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"Legs and feet — grounded, supported."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"Re-rate your intensity from 0 to 10. What number comes now?"

*(Pause 5 seconds)*

"Whatever it is — notice the direction it moved. Even a shift from 7 to 6 is significant. Your system heard you. It responded."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"Here are a few things you might notice in the hours and days ahead:"

- Deeper sleep than usual
- Emotional dreams
- A sense of lightness or spaciousness
- Old memories surfacing
- Irritability as your system detoxes

*(Pause 5 seconds)*

"This is all normal. Be gentle with yourself. Drink water. Rest when you need to. If emotions come up strong, tap again — or reach out to someone you trust."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"Bring your hands to your heart. Feel your own warmth. Say to yourself: 'I am here. I am doing the work. That is enough.'"

*(Pause 5 seconds)*

"Take a final breath together. In… hold… and out."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"When you're ready, gently open your eyes. Come back to the room, carrying with you whatever peace you've found today."

*(Pause 3 seconds)*

"You did important work today. Thank you."

---

**End of session.**

*This session is intended for self-guided emotional processing. If grief or trauma feels overwhelming, please seek support from a licensed therapist.*
