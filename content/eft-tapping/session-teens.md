# EFT Tapping Guided Session — Teens (14–18)

**Duration:** 7 minutes  
**Age Group:** Teens (14–18)  
**Focus:** School stress, social anxiety, exam pressure  
**Goal:** Reduce anxiety from a 5–8 down to 2–3 using EFT tapping

---

## Script

### Opening (0:00–0:45)

*(Warm, conversational, reassuring voice)*

"Hey. Take a breath with me."

*(Pause 3 seconds)*

"Breathe in… and out."

*(Pause 3 seconds)*

"Before we start tapping, think of something that's stressing you out right now. It could be a test tomorrow, a conversation you're dreading, or just that feeling of 'ugh, everything is too much.'"

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"Rate it from 0 to 10. Zero is totally fine. Ten is the worst it's ever felt. Just get a number."

*(Pause 3 seconds)*

"Got it? Great. Let's tap."

---

### Setup Statement (0:45–1:30)

"Find the side of your hand — the karate chop spot, like you're chopping wood."

*(Pause 2 seconds)*

"Tap that spot with 3 or 4 fingers, and repeat after me:"

*(Pace slows, voice softens)*

"Even though I'm feeling really stressed about [the thing], I accept how I'm feeling right now."

*(Pause 3 seconds)*

"Even though my mind is spinning and I don't know how to calm down, I'm okay exactly as I am."

*(Pause 3 seconds)*

"Even though this feels so big, I'm choosing to let some of it go now."

*(Pause 2 seconds)*

---

### The Tapping Sequence (1:30–5:30)

*(Voice becomes steady, rhythmic — moderate pace)*

"Now we're going to tap through each point. I'll guide you. Just tap each spot gently about 5–7 times as we go."

**Eyebrow:** *(tap tap tap)* "All this stress about [the thing]…"

**Side of Eye:** *(tap)* "Feeling it in my chest / stomach / head…"

**Under Eye:** *(tap)* "This worry that won't stop…"

**Under Nose:** *(tap)* "All this pressure…"

**Chin:** *(tap)* "I feel so overwhelmed…"

**Collarbone:** *(tap)* "This feeling of not being enough…"

**Under Arm:** *(tap)* "I just want to escape this…"

**Top of Head:** *(tap)* "All this stress…"

*(Pause 3 seconds — deep breath)*

"Good. Take a deep breath."

*(Pause 3 seconds)*

"Let's go again. This time, we're shifting toward acceptance."

**Eyebrow:** *(tap)* "Even with all this stress…"

**Side of Eye:** *(tap)* "I'm still here, doing my best…"

**Under Eye:** *(tap)* "I don't have to have it all figured out…"

**Under Nose:** *(tap)* "It's okay to feel this way…"

**Chin:** *(tap)* "I'm not alone in this…"

**Collarbone:** *(tap)* "I can get through this…"

**Under Arm:** *(tap)* "One step at a time…"

**Top of Head:** *(tap)* "I choose to let go of some of this pressure…"

*(Pause 4 seconds — deep breath)*

"Great. Check your number. Has it gone down?"

*(Pause 3 seconds)*

"One more round, focusing on relief."

**Eyebrow:** *(tap)* "This remaining stress…"

**Side of Eye:** *(tap)* "I'm allowed to feel better…"

**Under Eye:** *(tap)* "Releasing it now…"

**Under Nose:** *(tap)* "Letting it go…"

**Chin:** *(tap)* "I'm okay…"

**Collarbone:** *(tap)* "I can handle what comes…"

**Under Arm:** *(tap)* "I am more than this stress…"

**Top of Head:** *(tap)* "Peace. Just peace."

---

### Closing (5:30–7:00)

"Take a deep breath in… and out."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"Another one. In through your nose, out through your mouth."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"Check your number again. Notice if anything shifted."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"Whatever number it is, that's okay. You just did something really smart — you used your body to help your brain calm down."

*(Pause 3 seconds)*

"The more you tap, the easier it gets. Your brain starts to learn: 'Oh, when I tap, I can relax.'"

*(Pause 3 seconds)*

"Give yourself credit. You showed up. That's what matters."

*(Pause 2 seconds)*

"You've got this."

---

**End of session.**
