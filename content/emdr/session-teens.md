# EMDR Guided Session — Teens (14–18)\n\n**Duration:** 8 minutes  \n**Age Group:** Teens (14–18)  \n**Focus:** Exam stress & social anxiety  \n**Goal:** Reduce overwhelm around a specific school or social stressor\n\n---\n\n## Preparation Notes (for the app)\n\n- Ensure user is in a quiet, comfortable space\n- User should have their phone or tablet positioned so they can follow a dot/light across the screen (or use alternating audio tones via headphones)\n- Have water nearby\n- If at any point the user feels too overwhelmed, they can pause and use the grounding cue at the end\n\n---\n\n## Script\n\n### Phase 1: Setup & Grounding (0:00–1:30)\n\n*(Calm, warm, steady voice — slightly slower than normal speech)*\n\n\"Hi there. I'm glad you're here. Before we begin, take a deep breath with me.\"\n\n*(Pause 3 seconds)*\n\n\"Breathe in through your nose… and out through your mouth.\"\n\n*(Pause 3 seconds)*\n\n\"This is a safe space. Nothing you feel is wrong. We're just going to let your brain do what it already knows how to do — heal.\"\n\n*(Pause 2 seconds)*\n\n\"Think of something that's been on your mind lately. Maybe a test coming up. Maybe something someone said. Maybe just… everything feeling like too much. You don't have to tell me what it is. Just picture it — like a screenshot in your mind.\"\n\n*(Pause 5 seconds)*\n\n\"Notice where you feel it in your body. Your chest? Your stomach? Your shoulders? It's okay. Just notice.\"\n\n*(Pause 4 seconds)*\n\n\"Now rate how uncomfortable it feels, from 0 to 10. Zero means totally calm. Ten means the worst it could be. Just get a number in your head.\"\n\n*(Pause 4 seconds)*\n\n\"A negative thought that goes with this — like 'I'm not good enough' or 'I can't handle this' or 'Everyone's judging me.' Just let the words appear.\"\n\n*(Pause 3 seconds)*\n\n\"And a positive thought you'd rather believe. Something like 'I am enough' or 'I can get through this.' It doesn't have to feel true yet. We're going to help it feel true.\"\n\n*(Pause 3 seconds)*\n\n\"Okay. Let's begin.\"\n\n---\n\n### Phase 2: Desensitization (1:30–6:00)\n\n*(Voice remains calm, slightly rhythmic. The dot on screen begins moving left to right at about 1 cycle per second.)*\n\n\"Bring that memory to mind now. The image. The feeling in your body. The negative thought.\"\n\n*(Pause 3 seconds)*\n\n\"Just let it be there. And follow the light with your eyes. Left… right… left… right…\"\n\n*(Pause — let bilateral stimulation run for 20 seconds)*\n\n\"Good. Take a breath.\"\n\n*(Pause 4 seconds)*\n\n\"What are you noticing now? Any shift — in the image, your feelings, your body?\"\n\n*(Pause 5 seconds)*\n\n\"Just notice it. Don't try to change anything. And let's go again.\"\n\n*(Bilateral stimulation resumes for 25 seconds)*\n\n\"Breathe. Whatever came up is fine. That's your brain doing its work.\"\n\n*(Pause 3 seconds)*\n\n\"Let's go back to the original memory. Is it the same? Different? Farther away?\"\n\n*(Pause 5 seconds)*\n\n\"And follow the light…\"\n\n*(Bilateral stimulation for 25 seconds)*\n\n\"You're doing great. This takes courage. Take another breath.\"\n\n*(Pause 5 seconds)*\n\n\"Let's continue. Let your mind go wherever it needs to.\"\n\n*(Bilateral stimulation for 30 seconds)*\n\n\"Notice what's there now. It might be changing. That's okay. That's good.\"\n\n*(Bilateral stimulation for 20 seconds)*\n\n\"One more round. Stay with it.\"\n\n*(Bilateral stimulation for 20 seconds)*\n\n---\n\n### Phase 3: Installation (6:00–7:00)\n\n\"Take a deep breath in… and out.\"\n\n*(Pause 3 seconds)*\n\n\"Now bring up that positive thought we identified earlier. Say it to yourself.\"\n\n*(Pause 4 seconds)*\n\n\"As you hold that thought, follow the light again…\"\n\n*(Slow bilateral stimulation — 15 seconds)*\n\n\"On a scale from 0 to 10, how true does that positive thought feel now?\"\n\n*(Pause 4 seconds)*\n\n\"Let's strengthen it. Keep repeating the positive thought…\"\n\n*(Bilateral stimulation — 15 seconds)*\n\n---\n\n### Phase 4: Closure (7:00–8:00)\n\n\"We're going to wrap up now. Take a nice, deep breath.\"\n\n*(Pause 3 seconds)*\n\n\"Bring your hands to your chest or your belly. Feel your own warmth.\"\n\n*(Pause 3 seconds)*\n\n\"You just did something really brave. You faced something difficult, and you stayed with it. That matters.\"\n\n*(Pause 3 seconds)*\n\n\"When you're ready, gently open your eyes if they were closed. Look around the room. Name three things you can see.\"\n\n*(Pause 5 seconds)*\n\n\"Two things you can hear.\"\n\n*(Pause 3 seconds)*\n\n\"One thing you can feel — the ground under you, the air on your skin.\"\n\n*(Pause 3 seconds)*\n\n\"You're back here, in this room, safe and okay. Well done.\"\n\n*(Pause 2 seconds)*\n\n\"If anything came up that feels too big, you can talk to a trusted adult — a parent, a counselor, a teacher. You don't have to carry it alone.\"\n\n---\n\n*End of session.*", "filePath": "/home/team/shared/ener-g-t-49/content/emdr/session-teens.md"}
