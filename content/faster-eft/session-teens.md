# Faster EFT Guided Session — Teens (14–18)

**Duration:** 7 minutes  
**Age Group:** Teens (14–18)  
**Focus:** Social anxiety, test prep stress, feeling awkward or not fitting in  
**Goal:** Shift the felt sense of discomfort in the body and install a calmer baseline

---

## Script

### Opening (0:00–1:00)

*(Warm, casual, conversational — like a friend who gets it)*

"Hey. Let's do something quick that actually helps."

*(Pause 2 seconds)*

"Close your eyes for a second. Think about that thing that's making your stomach flip — maybe it's a test, or someone you have to talk to, or just the feeling of everything being kind of a lot."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"Got it? Great. Now instead of thinking about it, just notice: where do you feel it in your body? Your chest? Stomach? Shoulders? Throat?"

*(Pause 3 seconds)*

"Put your hand there. Just notice the sensation. Don't try to change it."

*(Pause 3 seconds)*

---

### Point 1: Find & Tap (1:00–2:30)

"Now start tapping on your collarbone point — right below the knot where your collarbones meet. Tap with 3 or 4 fingers, gently but steadily."

*(Pause 3 seconds)*

"Keep tapping and just say: 'I notice this feeling in my [chest / stomach / throat]. It's here. I don't have to change it. I'm just noticing it.'"

*(Pause 5 seconds)*

"Good. Now ask yourself: What shape is it? Is it tight? Heavy? Hot? Prickly? Like a knot? A weight? A buzz?"

*(Pause 5 seconds)*

"Don't overthink it. Whatever comes is fine."

*(Pause 3 seconds)*

---

### Point 2: The Shift (2:30–4:00)

"Now move your tapping to the side-of-eye point — the bone at the outer corner of your eye."

*(Pause 2 seconds)*

"And ask yourself: 'If this sensation had a color, what color would it be?'"

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"Now — 'If it could change color, what color would I rather it be?'"

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"'What would that feel like?' Just imagine it shifting."

*(Pause 5 seconds)*

"Now tap under your eye. Ask: 'What's changed? Is the sensation different? Smaller? Bigger? Moved somewhere else?'"

*(Pause 5 seconds)*

"Whatever it did — even if it seems like nothing — just notice."

*(Pause 3 seconds)*

---

### Point 3: Reframe (4:00–5:30)

"Tap on your karate chop point — the side of your hand."

*(Pause 2 seconds)*

"Say this to yourself: 'I've handled hard things before. I survived them. I can handle this too.'"

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"'This feeling doesn't control me. It's just a sensation passing through my body.'"

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"Now tap on the top of your head. Say: 'What if I could feel a little lighter right now? What if it's okay to feel good?'"

*(Pause 5 seconds)*

"Take a breath. Notice if anything shifted — even a tiny bit."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

---

### Check-In & Ground (5:30–7:00)

"Take a deep breath. In… and out."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"Rate it from 0 to 10 again. Where's it at now?"

*(Pause 3 seconds)*

"Whatever it is, that's progress. Your body just learned something: 'I can feel a hard feeling AND it can change.'"

*(Pause 3 seconds)*

"Name three things you can see in the room right now."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"Rub your hands together. Feel the warmth. You're here. You're okay."

*(Pause 3 seconds)*

"That's it. This whole thing took less time than scrolling social media, and it actually helped your nervous system. Nice work."

---

**End of session.**
