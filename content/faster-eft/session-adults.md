# Faster EFT Guided Session — Adults (37–65+)

**Duration:** 22 minutes  
**Age Group:** Adults (37–65+)  
**Focus:** Chronic stress patterns, long-held grief, life transitions (empty nest, retirement, aging), deep-seated beliefs  
**Goal:** Identify and process somatic layers of a long-standing issue, reset the emotional set point, install a new baseline

---

## Script

### Phase 1: Arriving & Intention (0:00–3:30)

*(Deep, unhurried, compassionate voice — creates spaciousness)*

"Welcome. I invite you to arrive fully. Not just physically — but with your whole attention. This is your time, and nothing else needs your focus right now."

*(Pause 5 seconds)*

"Find a comfortable position. If it feels right, close your eyes. Let your hands rest where they naturally fall."

*(Pause 5 seconds)*

"Take three slow, deep breaths — each one longer than the last. I'll guide you."

"Breathe in… and out."

*(Pause 6 seconds)*

"Deeper this time — fill your belly, your ribs, your chest. Hold for a moment… and release slowly."

*(Pause 7 seconds)*

"One more — the fullest breath yet. And exhale completely, letting go of everything you don't need to carry right now."

*(Pause 6 seconds)*

"Now, set an intention for this session. Not a goal you have to achieve — just a direction. Something like: 'I'm here to understand this pattern,' or 'I'm ready to release what I've been holding,' or 'I want to feel lighter.'"

*(Pause 8 seconds)*

"Hold that intention gently. We'll come back to it."

---

### Phase 2: Somatic Mapping (3:30–7:00)

"Bring to mind the issue or pattern you'd like to work with today. Something that has been present in your life for a while — weeks, months, maybe years."

*(Pause 8 seconds)*

"Don't tell the story. Instead, drop into your body and notice: where does this issue live in you physically?"

*(Pause 6 seconds)*

"Scan your body slowly. Notice any areas of density, tension, numbness, heat, coolness, or discomfort."

*(Pause 10 seconds)*

"When you find a sensation that seems connected to this issue, place your hand there. This is the body's way of saying 'this is relevant.'"

*(Pause 5 seconds)*

"Begin tapping on your collarbone point — steady, gentle, continuous."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"Ask yourself: 'If this sensation had a voice, what would it say? What word or phrase would it use to describe itself?'"

*(Pause 8 seconds)*

"'How old is this sensation? Is it from today? This year? From childhood?'"

*(Pause 8 seconds)*

"'What emotion or belief is woven into this sensation?'"

*(Pause 8 seconds)*

"Stay curious. Don't grab at answers. Let them surface in their own time."

*(Pause 5 seconds)*

---

### Phase 3: Dialoguing with the Sensation (7:00–12:00)

"Now we're going to dialogue with this sensation — not to get rid of it, but to understand it."

*(Pause 3 seconds)*

"Tap on your under-eye point and say: 'I see you. I feel you. You've been here for a while. Thank you for trying to protect me.'"

*(Pause 6 seconds)*

"'What are you protecting me from?'"

*(Pause 8 seconds)*

"'What would you be afraid would happen if you weren't here?'"

*(Pause 8 seconds)*

"Notice the answers that arise. Don't judge them. Just receive them."

*(Pause 5 seconds)*

"Now tap on your chin point and say: 'I understand your job was to keep me safe. But I'm not in danger anymore. I'm an adult now. I have resources. I have choices.'"

*(Pause 6 seconds)*

"'I can handle what comes. You don't have to work so hard anymore. You can rest.'"

*(Pause 6 seconds)*

"Tap on your side-of-eye point: 'What would you like me to know? Is there a message you've been holding for me?'"

*(Pause 10 seconds)*

"Take a breath. Let whatever came up settle."

---

### Phase 4: The Reframe (12:00–16:30)

"Now we introduce a new perspective. Not as a forced positive thought, but as a possibility."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"Tap on the karate chop point and say: 'Even though I've been carrying this pattern for so long, I can see it differently now. I am not this feeling. I am the one noticing the feeling.'"

*(Pause 6 seconds)*

"'The part of me that can notice the feeling — that part is whole. That part has never been broken.'"

*(Pause 6 seconds)*

"Tap on your collarbone point: 'What would it mean to live without this pattern? What would I gain? What would I have to grieve?'"

*(Pause 8 seconds)*

"'Both things can be true: I'm ready to release this, AND I honor what it's given me.'"

*(Pause 5 seconds)*

"Tap on the top of your head: 'I am updating my set point. Not through effort, but through awareness and acceptance.'"

*(Pause 5 seconds)*

"'My new set point is: [choose a word — peace, ease, presence, trust, calm, openness].'"

*(Pause 6 seconds)*

"Tap through the points one more time, repeating your new set-point word at each point."

**Eyebrow →** "Peace."
**Side of eye →** "Peace."
**Under eye →** "Peace."
**Under nose →** "Peace."
**Chin →** "Peace."
**Collarbone →** "Peace."
**Under arm →** "Peace."
**Top of head →** "Peace."

*(Pause 6 seconds)*

---

### Phase 5: Integration & Body Scan (16:30–20:00)

"Let your hands rest. Take a full, deep breath."

*(Pause 5 seconds)*

"Scan your body slowly from head to toe, noticing any changes."

"I invite you to notice with fresh eyes — what's different?"

*(Pause 6 seconds)*

"Crown of the head — any new sensations? Lightness? Tingling? Quiet?"

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"Face, jaw, throat — any softening?"

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"Shoulders, chest — release?"

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"Belly — deep, even breaths?"

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"Hips, lower back — letting go of stored tension?"

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"Legs, feet — grounded, supported?"

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"Rate the original issue from 0 to 10 now. What number arises?"

*(Pause 5 seconds)*

---

### Phase 6: Closing & Homework (20:00–22:00)

"You've done deep work today. This kind of processing touches the nervous system at a foundational level."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"In the hours and days ahead, you may notice:"

- Feeling unusually tired (your system is recalibrating)
- Vivid dreams or memories surfacing
- A sense of unfamiliar spaciousness
- Your old triggers don't hit as hard

*(Pause 6 seconds)*

"This is all part of the integration. Be kind to yourself. Rest if you need to. Hydrate well."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"Here's a simple practice to reinforce today's shift:"

"Each morning, tap on your collarbone for 30 seconds and repeat your new set-point word three times. This tells your nervous system: 'This is the new normal.'"

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"For tonight, place your hand on your heart before sleep and say: 'I'm learning to feel safe without this pattern. I trust myself through this transition.'"

*(Pause 5 seconds)*

"Take one final breath together. In… and out."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"When you're ready, gently open your eyes. Carry whatever peace you found here with you through the rest of your day."

*(Pause 3 seconds)*

"Thank you for your courage. Deep work takes deep bravery."

---

**End of session.**

*This session is for self-guided emotional wellness. For complex trauma or diagnosed conditions, please work with a licensed professional.*
