# TFT Tapping Guided Session — Young Adults (19–36)

**Duration:** 16 minutes  
**Age Group:** Young Adults (19–36)  
**Focus:** Work-related anxiety, financial worry, relationship stress, imposter syndrome  
**Goal:** Use the TFT anxiety algorithm with extended processing to reduce distress by 3+ points

---

## Script

### Phase 1: Setup & Centering (0:00–2:30)

*(Warm, professional voice — clear and unhurried)*

"Welcome. Let's begin by settling in. Take a comfortable seat. Close your eyes if that feels right."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"Take a slow breath — in through your nose, hold, and out through your mouth."

*(Pause 5 seconds)*

"Bring to mind a specific source of anxiety in your life right now. Not everything — just one thing. A work deadline, a financial decision, a relationship tension, an inner voice telling you you're not enough."

*(Pause 6 seconds)*

"Get a clear image or sense of it. Notice where you feel it in your body."

*(Pause 5 seconds)*

"Rate it from 0 to 10 — 0 being no disturbance, 10 being the worst imaginable."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"Hold that number. That's your baseline."

---

### Phase 2: Setup Statement (2:30–3:30)

"Find your karate chop point. Begin tapping steadily."

*(Pause 3 seconds)*

"Repeat after me, three times:"

"Even though I'm experiencing this anxiety about [the issue], I deeply and completely accept myself."

*(Pause 3 seconds)*

"Even though this anxiety feels very real and present, I accept my feelings without judgment."

*(Pause 3 seconds)*

"Even though my mind wants to fight or flee, I choose to be present with this process."

*(Pause 3 seconds)*

---

### Phase 3: The Algorithm — Round 1 (3:30–6:30)

"Now we run the TFT algorithm. Tap each point 5–7 times."

**Under Eye (UE):** *(tap)* "This anxiety in my [body location]…"

**Under Arm (UA):** *(tap)* "This tightness / heaviness / heat…"

**Collarbone (CB):** *(tap)* "All of this…"

*(Pause 3 seconds)*

"Now find the Gamut Point — on the back of your hand between the ring finger and pinky knuckles."

*(Pause 3 seconds)*

"Tap it continuously while we go through the 9-Gamut procedure."

*(Pace slows, guiding precisely)*

"1. Close your eyes…"
"2. Open your eyes…"
"3. Eyes down and to the right…"
"4. Eyes down and to the left…"
"5. Roll your eyes clockwise…"
"6. Roll your eyes counterclockwise…"
"7. Hum a few notes…"
"8. Count out loud from 1 to 5…"
"9. Hum again…"

*(Pause 3 seconds)*

"Stop tapping the Gamut. Take a breath."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"Now repeat the three-point sequence."

**Under Eye:** *(tap)* "This anxiety I'm noticing…"

**Under Arm:** *(tap)* "The part that's still holding on…"

**Collarbone:** *(tap)* "Releasing now…"

*(Pause 4 seconds — deep breath)*

---

### Phase 4: Check-In & Deeper Inquiry (6:30–9:30)

"Take a breath. Check your number. Has it moved?"

*(Pause 5 seconds)*

"Now we're going to go deeper. Bring the issue back to mind. Ask yourself: What's the core fear underneath this anxiety?"

*(Pause 6 seconds)*

"Is it fear of failure? Fear of rejection? Fear of being seen as incompetent? Fear of losing control?"

*(Pause 6 seconds)*

"Whatever it is, name it. This is the root."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"Now let's run the algorithm again, this time focused on that core fear."

*(Pause 3 seconds)*

**Karate Chop:** *(tap)* "Even though I'm afraid of [core fear], I accept this part of me."

**Under Eye:** *(tap)* "This fear of [core fear]…"

**Under Arm:** *(tap)* "I can feel it in my body…"

**Collarbone:** *(tap)* "This old familiar fear…"

*(Pause 2 seconds)*

"9-Gamut again — keep tapping:"

"1. Close… 2. Open… 3. Right… 4. Left… 5. Circle one way… 6. Circle the other… 7. Hum… 8. 1, 2, 3, 4, 5… 9. Hum…"

*(Pause 3 seconds)*

"Repeat the points:"

**Under Eye:** *(tap)* "Even with this fear…"

**Under Arm:** *(tap)* "I am capable…"

**Collarbone:** *(tap)* "I am safe enough to feel this and let it go…"

*(Pause 5 seconds)*

---

### Phase 5: Reframing & Installation (9:30–12:30)

"Now we install a new belief. What's the opposite of that core fear?"

*(Pause 5 seconds)*

"If the fear was 'I'm not good enough,' the new belief might be 'I am enough.' If the fear was 'I'll fail,' the new belief is 'I learn from every attempt.'"

*(Pause 5 seconds)*

"Got your new belief? Let's tap it in."

**Karate Chop:** *(tap)* "Even though I've carried this old belief, I choose to believe: [new belief]."

**Under Eye:** *(tap)* "[New belief]…"

**Under Arm:** *(tap)* "[New belief]…"

**Collarbone:** *(tap)* "[New belief]…"

*(Pause 2 seconds)*

"9-Gamut with the new belief: keep tapping while you do the movements, repeating your new belief silently."

*(Pause 8 seconds — guide through the 9 actions silently or with minimal cues)*

"Repeat the three points:"

**Under Eye:** *(tap)* "[New belief] is becoming true for me…"

**Under Arm:** *(tap)* "I can feel it…"

**Collarbone:** *(tap)* "Anchored…"

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

---

### Phase 6: Re-rate & Close (12:30–16:00)

"Let your hands rest. Take a deep breath."

*(Pause 5 seconds)*

"Rate your original issue from 0 to 10 now. What's the number?"

*(Pause 5 seconds)*

"Compare it to your starting number. Whatever the change — even one point — that's measurable shift. That's your energy system responding."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"Scan your body. What do you notice now? Any sensations that feel different — lighter, warmer, calmer?"

*(Pause 6 seconds)*

"Name three things you can see in the room."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"Two things you can hear."

*(Pause 3 seconds)*

"One thing you can feel."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"Here's what you might notice in the next few hours:"

- The issue feels less charged
- You find yourself breathing more deeply
- The same trigger comes up but doesn't hit as hard
- You remember the new belief more easily

*(Pause 5 seconds)*

"The TFT algorithm is designed to restore balance to your energy system. Each time you run it, the pattern weakens and the new pattern strengthens."

*(Pause 3 seconds)*

"Take one more breath. When you're ready, gently open your eyes."

*(Pause 3 seconds)*

"You did good work today."

---

**End of session.**
