# TFT Tapping Quick Reference Cheat Sheet

## Two-Minute Overview

Thought Field Therapy (TFT) is the original tapping modality — founded by Dr. Roger Callahan in the 1980s. It uses **specific algorithms** (sequences of tapping points) designed to rebalance the body's energy system when focused on a distressing thought.

---

### The Standard TFT Anxiety Algorithm

```
KC (setup) → UE → UA → CB → 9-Gamut → UE → UA → CB
```

| Step | Point | Taps | Action |
|------|-------|------|--------|
| 1 | **Karate Chop (KC)** | 5–7x | Repeat setup statement 3 times |
| 2 | **Under Eye (UE)** | 5–7x | Focus on the problem |
| 3 | **Under Arm (UA)** | 5–7x | Focus on the feeling |
| 4 | **Collarbone (CB)** | 5–7x | Deepening the release |
| 5 | **9-Gamut** | Continuous | 9 brain-hemisphere balancing actions |
| 6 | **Under Eye (UE)** | 5–7x | Repeat |
| 7 | **Under Arm (UA)** | 5–7x | Repeat |
| 8 | **Collarbone (CB)** | 5–7x | Repeat |

---

### The 9-Gamut Procedure

Tap the **Gamut Point** (back of hand, valley between ring & pinky knuckles) continuously while doing:

| # | Action | Purpose |
|---|--------|---------|
| 1 | Close eyes | Shift to internal focus |
| 2 | Open eyes | Return to external focus |
| 3 | Eyes down & to the right | Access visual memory |
| 4 | Eyes down & to the left | Access auditory memory |
| 5 | Roll eyes clockwise | Stimulate kinesthetic processing |
| 6 | Roll eyes counterclockwise | Balance hemispheres |
| 7 | Hum a tune | Engage right brain (music) |
| 8 | Count 1–5 | Engage left brain (numbers) |
| 9 | Hum again | Re-engage right brain, complete the circuit |

**Why:** This alternates between brain hemispheres and brain functions, helping the energy shift integrate.

### Psychological Reversal

A block in the energy system that prevents healing. **Symptoms:**
- Number won't drop after 2 algorithm rounds
- You feel more resistant to the idea of healing
- You keep thinking "this won't work"

**How to clear it:**
Tap KC and say 3 times:
*"Even though some part of me doesn't want to let this go, I deeply and completely accept myself."*

Then re-run the algorithm.

---

### Collarbone Breathing

A TFT technique to "check" the energy system:

1. Tap CB continuously
2. Take a full breath and hold
3. While holding, tap CB and ask: "Is there anything else?"
4. Exhale
5. If a new issue arises, address it

---

### Tapping Tips

- Use 2–3 fingertips, firm but not painful
- Tap 5–7 times per point (except Gamut — tap continuously)
- Breathe between rounds
- Stay focused on the issue while tapping
- Re-rate after each algorithm round

---

### When to Run the Algorithm

- Daily for chronic anxiety (once in the morning)
- As needed for acute stress
- Before triggering events (meetings, exams, conversations)
- At bedtime for racing thoughts

---

### Research Notes

- Developed by Dr. Roger Callahan (1980s)
- 60–90% success rate for phobias in original clinical reports (Callahan & Trubo, 2001)
- Controlled studies show significant reduction in phobic anxiety (Sakai et al., 2001)
- Used by some VA hospitals and trauma centers
