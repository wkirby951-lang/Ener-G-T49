# TFT Tapping Guided Session — Teens (14–18)

**Duration:** 8 minutes  
**Age Group:** Teens (14–18)  
**Focus:** Test anxiety, fear of speaking up, worry spirals  
**Goal:** Use the TFT anxiety algorithm to reduce acute stress

---

## Script

### Opening (0:00–1:00)

*(Friendly, clear, energetic voice)*

"Hey! Ready to try something that might feel a little weird at first but actually works? Great."

*(Pause 2 seconds)*

"Take a breath. Think about what's stressing you out right now — a test, a conversation, a situation. Rate it 0 to 10."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"We're going to follow a specific sequence — it's like a recipe for your nervous system. Just tap each spot about 5 times when I say."

---

### Step 1: Setup (1:00–1:30)

"Find your karate chop spot — side of your hand."

*(Pause 2 seconds)*

"Tap it and say this with me: 'Even though I feel this [anxiety / worry / stress], I accept how I'm feeling right now.'"

*(Repeat 3 times)*

---

### Step 2: The Algorithm Sequence (1:30–3:00)

*(Rhythmic, steady pace)*

"Now we tap through three specific points. This is the TFT anxiety sequence."

**Under Eye:** *(tap 5 times)* "This anxiety I'm feeling…"

**Under Arm:** *(tap 5 times)* "This tightness in my body…"

**Collarbone:** *(tap 5 times)* "All this stress…"

*(Pause 2 seconds)*

"Good. Now find the Gamut Point — it's on the back of your hand, in the valley between your ring finger and pinky knuckles."

*(Pause 3 seconds)*

---

### Step 3: The 9-Gamut (3:00–5:00)

"Tap the Gamut Point continuously while we do these 9 things. Keep tapping the whole time."

"1. Close your eyes…"

*(Pause 2 seconds)*

"2. Open your eyes…"

*(Pause 2 seconds)*

"3. Eyes down and to the right…"

*(Pause 2 seconds)*

"4. Eyes down and to the left…"

*(Pause 2 seconds)*

"5. Roll your eyes in a circle — one direction…"

*(Pause 3 seconds)*

"6. Roll your eyes the other direction…"

*(Pause 3 seconds)*

"7. Hum a few notes — anything, like your favorite song…"

*(Pause 3 seconds)*

"8. Count out loud: 1… 2… 3… 4… 5"

*(Pause 3 seconds)*

"9. Hum a few more notes…"

*(Pause 3 seconds)*

"You can stop tapping on the Gamut Point now. Take a breath."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

---

### Step 4: Repeat the Sequence (5:00–6:00)

"Now we repeat the first three points."

**Under Eye:** *(tap 5 times)* "Even with all that stress…"

**Under Arm:** *(tap 5 times)* "I'm still here…"

**Collarbone:** *(tap 5 times)* "I'm doing this…"

*(Pause 2 seconds)*

**Under Eye:** "Releasing the rest…"

**Under Arm:** "Letting it go…"

**Collarbone:** "Feeling lighter…"

---

### Step 5: Re-rate & Close (6:00–8:00)

"Take a deep breath in… and out."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"Rate it from 0 to 10 now. What number's there?"

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"Notice if anything shifted — even a little. That's the algorithm working."

*(Pause 3 seconds)*

"Here's the thing about TFT: the sequence is designed to match exactly what your energy system needs. It doesn't matter if you believe it will work. Your body knows the recipe."

*(Pause 3 seconds)*

"Name three things you see around you."

*(Pause 4 seconds)*

"Wiggle your fingers and toes. You're good."

*(Pause 2 seconds)*

"If your number is still high, you can run the algorithm again tomorrow. The more you do it, the faster your system learns to let go."

*(Pause 2 seconds)*

"Well done. Seriously."

---

**End of session.**
