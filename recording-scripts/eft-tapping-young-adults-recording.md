## Narration Script

[Warm, professional, grounded voice]

Welcome. Take a moment to arrive. Settle into your seat. Feet flat on the floor. Hands resting in your lap.

[pause 4s]

Take a deep breath. In through your nose — fill your lungs completely. And exhale slowly through your mouth, making a soft sigh sound.

[pause 4s]

Now, bring to mind a specific stressor that's been weighing on you. Something that's been showing up in your thoughts — a work situation, a relationship dynamic, a financial worry, a decision you're struggling with.

[pause 5s]

Describe it briefly to yourself. What's the situation? What feelings come up? What negative belief is attached?

[pause 6s]

Now rate the intensity of this stressor — 0 being no disturbance at all, 10 being the most intense you can imagine. Just get a number.

[pause 4s]

Let's begin tapping. Locate your karate chop point — the fleshy side of your hand, between your pinky finger and your wrist.

---

[slower, intentional]

Tap the karate chop point gently but continuously. We'll say the setup statement together.

Even though I'm carrying this stress about [the issue], I deeply and completely accept myself.

[pause 4s]

Even though I feel anxious, overwhelmed, frustrated, scared, or hurt, I accept all of my feelings right now. They're valid.

[pause 4s]

Even though this situation feels stuck or heavy, I'm open to seeing it differently. I'm open to feeling better.

[pause 3s]

---

[rhythmic, steady]

We'll now tap through all eight points. Repeat the reminder phrase after me, or just say it in your mind.

Eyebrow — This stress about [the issue]…

Side of eye — Feeling it in my body…

Under eye — All these emotions…

Under nose — This heaviness in my chest…

Chin — The stories I keep telling myself…

Collarbone — This feeling of not enough, stuck, alone, overwhelmed…

Under arm — All this tension…

Top of head — This whole situation…

[pause 4s]

[deep breath pause]

---

[empathetic, acknowledging]

Let's go deeper. This round, we acknowledge the resistance.

Karate chop — Even though some part of me doesn't want to let this go, I accept that too.

Eyebrow — I don't know if I can let this go…

Side of eye — What if I'm supposed to hold onto this worry?

Under eye — It feels unsafe to relax…

Under nose — This stress has been protecting me…

Chin — I'm learning I can be safe AND relaxed…

Collarbone — I release the tension I don't need…

Under arm — Letting go of control, just a little…

Top of head — I'm safe. I'm capable. I can handle what comes.

[pause 5s]

[lightening slightly]

Notice any shifts. Maybe a deep breath. A yawn. A sigh. These are signs of release.

[pause 3s]

---

[reframing, hopeful]

This round, we introduce a new possibility.

Eyebrow — Even with this challenge…

Side of eye — I'm still here, still capable…

Under eye — I've handled hard things before…

Under nose — I can handle this too…

Chin — I'm doing my best with what I have…

Collarbone — It doesn't have to be perfect…

Under arm — I give myself permission to feel better…

Top of head — I choose peace. I choose clarity.

[pause 4s]

Karate chop — Even though this situation hasn't fully resolved, I trust myself to navigate it. I choose to feel less burdened right now.

[pause 3s]

Eyebrow — This remaining tension…

Side of eye — Releasing it now…

Under eye — My body deserves rest…

Under nose — My mind deserves peace…

Chin — I am worthy of relief…

Collarbone — I'm not my stress…

Under arm — I am more than this problem…

Top of head — Clear mind. Open heart. Steady breath.

[pause 5s]

---

[grounding, closing]

Take a full, deep breath. Let your hands rest.

[pause 5s]

Re-rate your intensity level from 0 to 10. What number came up?

[pause 5s]

Whatever shift you experienced — even one or two points — that's real. That's your nervous system responding.

[pause 4s]

Let's do a quick body scan starting from your head…

[slow, with pauses]

Jaw — is it tight? Let it soften.

[pause 4s]

Shoulders — let them drop.

[pause 4s]

Chest and belly — soft. Breathing.

[pause 4s]

Hips and legs — letting gravity hold them.

[pause 4s]

Name three things you can see in the room.

[pause 5s]

Notice how your body feels compared to when we started. Even a small shift matters.

[pause 4s]

This is a practice. Each time you tap, you're teaching your nervous system that it's safe to let go. The more you practice, the faster the shifts come.

[pause 3s]

If the number is still above 5, you might benefit from another round on the same issue later, or breaking it into smaller parts to tap on, one at a time.

[pause 3s]

[warm, sincere]

Thank you for showing up for yourself. Take another breath. And when you're ready, gently open your eyes.

[END]
