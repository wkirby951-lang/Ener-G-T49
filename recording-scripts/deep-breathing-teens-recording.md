## Narration Script

[Warm, clear, relaxed voice — not too slow, not too fast]

Hey. Stop what you're doing for a minute. Seriously. This will take less time than one TikTok.

[pause 2s]

Find a seat or a spot where you can just be for a few minutes. Doesn't have to be perfect.

[pause 3s]

Let your hands rest wherever they're comfortable. If you want, close your eyes. If not, just look at a spot on the floor.

[pause 3s]

Take one breath with me — just breathe in… and out.

[pause 4s]

Good. That's already different from 10 seconds ago.

[pause 2s]

We're going to try something called Box Breathing. It's what Navy SEALs use to stay calm under pressure. No joke.

[pause 2s]

A box has four equal sides. We're going to breathe in four equal parts. Inhale for 4 counts, hold for 4, exhale for 4, hold for 4 — and repeat.

[pause 3s]

Let's do it together.

Breathe in… 2… 3… 4.

[pause 4s]

Hold… 2… 3… 4.

[pause 4s]

Breathe out… 2… 3… 4.

[pause 4s]

Hold… 2… 3… 4.

[pause 4s]

One more round — In… 2… 3… 4. Hold… 2… 3… 4. Out… 2… 3… 4. Hold… 2… 3… 4.

[pause 6s]

Third round — In… hold… out… hold… You're getting the rhythm.

[pause 6s]

Notice how your body feels. Calmer? Lighter? That's your nervous system saying "thank you."

[pause 3s]

Now let's switch to another technique — 4-7-8 breathing. It's great for when you're trying to fall asleep or when anxiety hits.

[pause 2s]

Inhale for 4 counts. Hold for 7. Exhale for 8. The long exhale is what triggers the relaxation response.

[pause 3s]

Let's try it.

Breathe in… 2… 3… 4.

[pause 4s]

Hold… 2… 3… 4… 5… 6… 7.

[pause 7s]

Slow exhale… 2… 3… 4… 5… 6… 7… 8.

[pause 8s]

One more — In… 2… 3… 4. Hold… 2… 3… 4… 5… 6… 7. Out… 2… 3… 4… 5… 6… 7… 8.

[pause 8s]

Last one — In… hold… and all the way out, letting everything go.

[pause 8s]

Now just breathe normally. Let your breath find its natural rhythm.

[pause 5s]

Notice how different your body feels compared to when we started.

[pause 4s]

Your shoulders lower? Your jaw less tight? Your mind quieter?

[pause 4s]

That's the power of your breath. It's free, it's always with you, and it's the fastest way to reset your nervous system.

[pause 3s]

Next time you feel stress building — before a test, after a hard conversation, in the middle of a panic — try 5 rounds of box breathing. It takes 60 seconds and it works.

[pause 3s]

Good job. You just took care of yourself.

[END]
