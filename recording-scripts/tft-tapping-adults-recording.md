## Narration Script

[Deep, centering, spacious voice — generous pauses throughout]

Welcome. I invite you to arrive fully in this moment. Nothing else needs your attention right now.

[pause 5s]

Sit comfortably. Let your spine be tall but not rigid. Rest your hands wherever they're comfortable.

[pause 5s]

Take three slow, complete breaths. I'll guide you.

First breath — in through your nose, filling your belly… and slowly out.

[pause 6s]

Second breath — deeper now. Fill your belly, your ribs, your chest… hold at the top… and release with a soft sigh.

[pause 7s]

Third breath — the fullest yet. Breathe in everything you need… and breathe out everything you're ready to release.

[pause 7s]

Now, bring to mind the issue you'd like to work with today. It may be a specific situation, a recurring pattern, a long-held worry, or a grief that hasn't fully moved.

[pause 8s]

Don't dive into the story. Instead, just notice — when you bring this to mind, what do you feel in your body? Where is the sensation located?

[pause 6s]

Rate the intensity from 0 to 10. This is your starting point.

[pause 5s]

Set a gentle intention for this session — not a demand, but a direction. Something like — "I'm ready to release what no longer serves me," or "I'm open to seeing this differently."

[pause 6s]

---

In TFT, we first check for psychological reversal — an energetic block where some part of you resists the healing. We'll clear it now.

[pause 3s]

Find your karate chop point and tap steadily.

[pause 3s]

Say this statement three times, with sincerity.

Even though there's a part of me that doesn't want to let go of this anxiety, I deeply and completely accept myself.

[pause 4s]

Even though a part of me believes this worry is keeping me safe, I'm open to releasing what I no longer need.

[pause 4s]

Even though I've carried this pattern for years and it's become familiar — even comfortable — I choose to update my energy system now.

[pause 4s]

This clears the block. Now we can proceed.

---

Now we run the TFT anxiety algorithm. Tap each point 5 to 7 times.

Under eye — "This heavy feeling I've been carrying…"

Under arm — "The weight of years of worry…"

Collarbone — "All of this stored tension…"

[pause 3s]

Find the Gamut Point — the valley between the ring and pinky knuckles on the back of your hand.

[pause 3s]

Tap it continuously as we go through the 9-Gamut.

One — gently close your eyes.
Two — open your eyes.
Three — look down and to the right.
Four — look down and to the left.
Five — roll your eyes in a full circle clockwise.
Six — roll your eyes counterclockwise.
Seven — hum a few notes — any tune.
Eight — count out loud from 1 to 5.
Nine — hum again.

[pause 3s]

Release the Gamut. Take a full breath.

[pause 5s]

Repeat the three-point sequence.

Under eye — "Whatever shifted, I welcome it…"

Under arm — "My energy system is balancing…"

Collarbone — "Releasing… releasing… releasing…"

[pause 5s]

Take a breath. Check your number. What is it now?

[pause 5s]

If it dropped, good — the algorithm is working. If it stayed the same or went up, that's also information — there's a deeper layer we need to address.

[pause 4s]

Bring the issue back to mind. This time, ask — "What's the earliest memory connected to this feeling?"

[pause 10s]

"How old was I when I first learned to feel this way?"

[pause 8s]

"What belief did I form about myself back then?"

[pause 8s]

Let the answer come. It might be a specific memory, or just a sense.

[pause 5s]

We're going to run the algorithm again, this time focused on that younger self and that old belief.

[pause 4s]

Karate chop — "Even though I've been carrying this belief since I was [age], I release the part of me that's been stuck there. I accept that younger version of me completely."

[pause 5s]

Under eye — "That old belief — [the belief]…"

Under arm — "The weight I've carried since childhood…"

Collarbone — "This pattern that's been running so long…"

[pause 3s]

Gamut Point — 9-Gamut again.

One — close. Two — open. Three — right. Four — left. Five — circle one way. Six — circle the other. Seven — hum. Eight — count to 5. Nine — hum.

[pause 3s]

Repeat the three points.

Under eye — "I am not that belief…"

Under arm — "I am the one noticing the belief…"

Collarbone — "And I am free to choose differently now…"

[pause 5s]

---

What belief would you like to carry forward instead? Choose words that feel true — or that you'd like to feel true.

[pause 6s]

Let's install it.

Karate chop — "Even though the old pattern was familiar, I choose [new belief]. I accept myself in this new way."

[pause 4s]

Under eye — "[New belief] is becoming my truth…"

Under arm — "I can feel it settling in…"

Collarbone — "Anchored in my body and energy system…"

[pause 3s]

Gamut Point with the new belief — keep tapping through the 9-Gamut while silently repeating your new belief.

[pause 8s]

Repeat the three points.

Under eye — "[New belief]…"

Under arm — "This is my new baseline…"

Collarbone — "Integrated."

[pause 5s]

---

Let your hands rest completely. Take a long, deep breath.

[pause 6s]

Scan your body now, as if you're meeting it for the first time.

Crown of your head — any sensations?

[pause 4s]

Face, jaw, throat — any softening?

[pause 4s]

Shoulders, chest — what do they feel like now?

[pause 4s]

Belly — rising and falling with ease?

[pause 4s]

Hips — the seat of your deep holding. What's here?

[pause 5s]

Legs, feet — grounded and supported?

[pause 4s]

Re-rate the intensity of your original issue from 0 to 10.

[pause 5s]

Notice the direction of change. Even a shift of 1 or 2 points represents a real change in your energy system.

[pause 4s]

In the coming days, you may notice feeling unusually calm or quiet, old worries seem less compelling, memories surfacing with less emotional charge, or a sense of unfamiliar peace.

[pause 6s]

To reinforce today's work, tap your three collarbone points for 30 seconds each morning this week while repeating your new belief.

[pause 4s]

Place one hand on your heart. Say to yourself — "I did something courageous today. I faced an old pattern and chose to update it. That matters."

[pause 6s]

Take one final breath together. In… hold… and out.

[pause 5s]

When you're ready, gently open your eyes. Carry the peace you found here with you.

[pause 3s]

Thank you for your trust and your courage.

[END]
