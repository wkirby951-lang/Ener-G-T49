## Narration Script

[Deep, unhurried, compassionate voice — generous pauses throughout]

Hello. Thank you for being here with yourself today. This is your time.

[pause 4s]

Find a comfortable seated position. If you can, sit upright enough to stay alert, but relaxed enough to feel at ease. Let your hands rest wherever they naturally fall.

[pause 5s]

Take three slow, deep breaths. I'll count you through them.

[pause 2s]

First breath — in through your nose… 1… 2… 3… 4… and out through your mouth… 1… 2… 3… 4… 5… 6.

[pause 5s]

Second breath — in… hold… and longer out.

[pause 6s]

Third breath — let your belly soften. In… and all the way out.

[pause 5s]

Now, bring to mind something you'd like to work with today. This could be a recurring stressor that drains your energy, a grief that hasn't fully been processed, a life transition — retirement, empty nest, aging, health changes — a pattern you've noticed — people-pleasing, self-criticism, worry — or a specific memory that still carries emotional charge.

[pause 10s]

Let it surface naturally. Don't force it. Trust whatever shows up.

[pause 8s]

Now, rate this issue's intensity from 0 to 10 — where 0 is no disturbance at all, and 10 is the most overwhelming you can imagine. Don't overthink the number. Your first instinct is usually right.

[pause 5s]

---

[slower, intentional]

Find your karate chop point. The fleshy side of your hand.

[pause 2s]

Begin tapping there, gently but steadily.

[pause 3s]

Let's say the setup statement together. I'll say it first — you can repeat it aloud or silently.

Even though I've been carrying this weight for a long time — maybe years — I choose to release some of it now. I deeply and completely accept myself.

[pause 5s]

Even though this feeling is familiar — it's almost like an old friend I don't know how to live without — I'm open to letting some of it go today.

[pause 5s]

Even though a part of me is scared of what might be underneath this feeling, I trust that I am ready to process whatever comes. I am safe enough to heal.

[pause 4s]

---

[rhythmic, steady — compassionate]

We'll tap through the points now. Say the reminder phrase or hold the feeling in your mind.

Eyebrow — This heavy feeling I've been carrying…

Side of eye — This weight in my chest, shoulders, belly…

Under eye — All these years of holding on…

Under nose — This familiar ache…

Chin — This story I've been telling myself…

Collarbone — This fear of letting go…

Under arm — All the unshed tears…

Top of head — This deep, quiet knowing that something needs to shift…

[pause 5s]

Good. Take a breath. Notice what surfaced — an image, a word, a sensation.

Karate chop — Even though there's resistance, I honor it. It's been protecting me. I'm safe enough to explore.

[pause 4s]

Eyebrow — This resistance…

Side of eye — It's okay to take my time…

Under eye — I don't have to fix everything today…

Under nose — Just one layer, one moment at a time…

Chin — I give myself permission to feel…

Collarbone — Permission to release…

Under arm — Permission to heal at my own pace…

Top of head — I am held. I am safe. I am ready.

[pause 6s]

---

[softening — grief work]

Now we'll work with what came up. If grief, sadness, or loss surfaced, this round is for you.

Karate chop — Even though I've been holding onto this grief, I give myself permission to grieve fully. I am not broken for feeling this way.

[pause 4s]

Eyebrow — This sadness that lives in my body…

Side of eye — The tears I haven't shed…

Under eye — The person, or version of life, I've lost…

Under nose — This ache of things left unsaid…

Chin — The weight of unexpressed love…

Collarbone — I honor this grief. It matters.

Under arm — It's not too much. I'm not too much.

Top of head — Grief is love with nowhere to go. I choose to let love remain.

[pause 6s]

Take a moment. Let whatever needs to flow, flow. There's no rush.

[pause 8s]

Continue tapping wherever feels right, or follow me through the points again.

Eyebrow — I give this grief space…

Side of eye — I don't need to rush past it…

Under eye — Healing happens at its own speed…

Under nose — I'm learning to be tender with myself…

Chin — I am resilient AND I am gentle…

Collarbone — Both can be true…

Under arm — I am still whole, even in my grief…

Top of head — Peace. Deep, quiet peace.

[pause 6s]

---

[reframing, integrating]

Now we move toward integration. What have you learned from this experience? What strength did you discover you didn't know you had?

[pause 6s]

Let's tap in the new story.

Karate chop — Even though I've been shaped by this experience, I am not defined by it. I am more than what happened to me.

[pause 4s]

Eyebrow — I choose to see myself with compassion…

Side of eye — I did the best I could with what I knew…

Under eye — And now I know more, so I can do better…

Under nose — For myself.

Chin — I release the need to be who I was before…

Collarbone — I welcome who I'm becoming…

Under arm — Wisdom comes through experience, not despite it…

Top of head — I am not broken. I am healing.

[pause 5s]

Eyebrow — This new story feels…

Side of eye — A little unfamiliar, but true…

Under eye — I am allowed to feel lighter…

Under nose — I am allowed to feel joy again…

Chin — I am allowed to rest…

Collarbone — This is not a betrayal of the past…

Under arm — This is honoring how far I've come…

Top of head — I claim my peace. I claim my wholeness.

[pause 6s]

---

[grounding, spacious closing]

Let your hands rest. Take a full, slow breath.

[pause 5s]

Scan your body from the crown of your head to the soles of your feet.

Top of your head… any sensations?

[pause 4s]

Forehead, eyes, jaw — soft?

[pause 4s]

Neck and shoulders — letting go?

[pause 4s]

Chest and heart — what do you feel here now?

[pause 5s]

Belly — full and soft.

[pause 4s]

Hips — the seat of your resilience.

[pause 4s]

Legs and feet — grounded, supported.

[pause 4s]

Re-rate your intensity from 0 to 10. What number comes now?

[pause 5s]

Whatever it is — notice the direction it moved. Even a shift from 7 to 6 is significant. Your system heard you. It responded.

[pause 4s]

Here are a few things you might notice in the hours and days ahead: deeper sleep than usual, emotional dreams, a sense of lightness or spaciousness, old memories surfacing, or irritability as your system detoxes.

[pause 5s]

This is all normal. Be gentle with yourself. Drink water. Rest when you need to. If emotions come up strong, tap again — or reach out to someone you trust.

[pause 4s]

[warm, affirming]

Bring your hands to your heart. Feel your own warmth. Say to yourself — "I am here. I am doing the work. That is enough."

[pause 5s]

Take a final breath together. In… hold… and out.

[pause 4s]

When you're ready, gently open your eyes. Come back to the room, carrying with you whatever peace you've found today.

[pause 3s]

You did important work today. Thank you.

[END]
