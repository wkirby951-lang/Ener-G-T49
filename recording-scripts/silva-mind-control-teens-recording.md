## Narration Script

[Calm, steady, engaging voice — not too slow or too fast]

Hey. Find a comfortable position — sitting or lying down. Close your eyes if you're okay with that.

[pause 3s]

We're going to count down from 10 to 1. Each number, you relax a little more. By the time we reach 1, you'll be in a calm, focused state — your brain in the perfect learning zone.

[pause 3s]

Here we go.

10 — Relax your feet and legs. Let them feel heavy and warm.

[pause 4s]

9 — Relax your stomach and lower back. Let your belly soften.

[pause 4s]

8 — Relax your chest and shoulders. Let them drop.

[pause 4s]

7 — Relax your arms and hands. Let them rest completely.

[pause 4s]

6 — Relax your neck and jaw. Unclench your teeth.

[pause 4s]

5 — Relax your face — your forehead, your eyes, your cheeks.

[pause 4s]

4 — Your whole body is relaxed now. Warm. Heavy. Comfortable.

[pause 4s]

3 — Your mind is quieting. Thoughts floating by like clouds.

[pause 4s]

2 — You're drifting into a deeper level of calm.

[pause 4s]

1 — You're at the alpha level now. Deeply relaxed and fully aware. This is the perfect state for learning, creating, and healing.

[pause 5s]

Imagine a screen in front of you — like a movie screen, but inside your mind. It can be any color you like. Let's start with a calming blue.

[pause 4s]

See the blue screen. Feel the calm.

[pause 4s]

Now, picture something you're nervous about — maybe a test, a presentation, or a conversation you have coming up.

[pause 5s]

On your mental screen, imagine yourself doing it and doing it well. See yourself calm, confident, and capable.

[pause 6s]

See it in detail — what you're wearing, where you are, the expression on your face — calm and focused.

[pause 6s]

Now add feeling. Feel the confidence in your body. Feel the calm in your breath. Feel the focus in your mind.

[pause 6s]

Make the image brighter. Let the colors be vivid. This is your future, and you're seeing it clearly.

[pause 5s]

Now, repeat these words silently to yourself.

"I am calm. I am capable. I am prepared."

[pause 5s]

"I trust myself. I can handle whatever comes."

[pause 5s]

"My brain knows what to do."

[pause 5s]

Let these words sink into your mind like seeds being planted.

[pause 4s]

Now we'll come back up, slowly and gently.

[pause 2s]

1… 2… 3 — feeling more awake…

[pause 3s]

4… 5… 6 — moving your fingers and toes…

[pause 3s]

7… 8… 9 — taking a deeper breath…

[pause 3s]

10 — eyes open, alert, calm, and clear.

[pause 3s]

Notice how you feel. That relaxed calm is your alpha state. The more you practice it, the easier it is to access — even in the middle of a test or a stressful moment.

[pause 3s]

Bonus tip — next time you're about to start something stressful, touch your thumb, index, and middle finger together. That's your signal to drop into that calm, focused alpha state instantly.

[END]
