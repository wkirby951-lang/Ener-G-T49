## Narration Script

[Warm, grounded, conversational — a guide rather than an instructor]

Welcome. Take a seat and let yourself settle. This is a different kind of tapping session — less repetition, more curiosity.

[pause 4s]

Take one deep breath together. In through your nose… and out through your mouth with a sigh.

[pause 4s]

Now bring to mind something that's been tugging at you. A situation, a recurring thought, a relationship dynamic, a decision you've been avoiding.

[pause 5s]

Don't analyze it. Just notice — when you think about this, where do you feel it in your body? Is there a sensation somewhere? Tightness, heaviness, hollowness, pressure, heat?

[pause 6s]

Put one hand on that spot. Just acknowledge it. No need to change it. We're going to work with it directly.

[pause 4s]

Start tapping on your collarbone point. Gentle, steady taps.

[pause 3s]

Keep tapping and say to yourself — "I notice this sensation in my body. It's here. I'm not trying to get rid of it. I'm just curious about it."

[pause 5s]

Now ask yourself these questions, one at a time, pausing after each one.

If this sensation had a shape, what shape would it be?

[pause 6s]

What texture?

[pause 5s]

What temperature?

[pause 5s]

If it could speak, what would it say?

[pause 8s]

Don't force the answers. Just let them come. Often the first thing that pops in is the truth.

[pause 4s]

Now shift your tapping to the side-of-eye point.

[pause 2s]

Ask — what's the emotion underneath this sensation? Is there a specific feeling — anger, fear, sadness, shame, grief, frustration?

[pause 6s]

And what's underneath that emotion? What belief about yourself or the world is underneath?

[pause 8s]

Great. You've identified the root layer. Now we work with it directly.

[pause 3s]

Tap on your under-eye point and say — "I notice this belief. I've been carrying this for a while. It's familiar."

[pause 5s]

"But familiar doesn't mean true. I'm open to the possibility that this belief is not the whole truth."

[pause 5s]

Tap on your chin point. "What would it be like to hold this belief more lightly? To question it, just for a moment?"

[pause 6s]

"If this belief wasn't true — what would be possible?"

[pause 8s]

Notice what shifts in your body as you ask that. Any movement, release, expansion, softening.

[pause 5s]

Tap on your collarbone again and say — "I'm choosing a new possibility. I don't have to fully believe it yet. I'm just opening the door."

[pause 5s]

"The new possibility is — [your new belief]."

[pause 6s]

Tap on the top of your head. "I'm updating my emotional set point. Not through force. Through awareness."

[pause 4s]

Let your hands rest. Take a full breath.

[pause 5s]

Check in with your body. Is the original sensation still there? Different size? Different location? Different quality?

[pause 6s]

Rate it from 0 to 10 now.

[pause 4s]

If the number dropped by 2 or more, that's excellent. If it stayed the same or went up, that's not a failure — it means there's a deeper layer we can work with next time.

[pause 4s]

Let's do one short round to anchor the shift.

[pause 3s]

Tap on your eyebrow — "This shift I've made…"

Side of eye — "My body knows how to release…"

Under eye — "I trust this process…"

Under nose — "I'm learning to feel AND let go…"

Chin — "Both at the same time…"

Collarbone — "I am capable of change…"

Under arm — "My set point is recalibrating…"

Top of head — "Peace. Clarity. Ease."

[pause 5s]

Take another deep breath. Let your hands rest on your thighs.

[pause 4s]

Scan your body one more time — head to toe. Notice what feels different from when we started.

[pause 6s]

Name one thing that shifted — even if it's subtle.

[pause 5s]

This is your new data point. Your nervous system is learning that this feeling doesn't have to be permanent. It can move. It can change.

[pause 4s]

Over the next few hours and days, you might notice the issue feels less heavy, you remember the insight you had today, your body takes a deeper breath than usual, or the same old trigger doesn't hit as hard.

[pause 5s]

All of these are signs of your set point shifting. Trust them.

[pause 3s]

If you want to reinforce this, tap on the collarbone for 30 seconds tonight before bed and repeat your new belief.

[pause 3s]

Thank you for your curiosity and courage. Take one more breath. When you're ready, open your eyes.

[END]
