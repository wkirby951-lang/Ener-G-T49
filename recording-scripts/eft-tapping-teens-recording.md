## Narration Script

[Warm, conversational, reassuring voice]

Hey. Take a breath with me.

[pause 3s]

Breathe in… and out.

[pause 3s]

Before we start tapping, think of something that's stressing you out right now. It could be a test tomorrow, a conversation you're dreading, or just that feeling of "ugh, everything is too much."

[pause 4s]

Rate it from 0 to 10. Zero is totally fine. Ten is the worst it's ever felt. Just get a number.

[pause 3s]

Got it? Great. Let's tap.

---

Find the side of your hand — the karate chop spot, like you're chopping wood.

[pause 2s]

Tap that spot with three or four fingers, and repeat after me:

[slower, softer]

Even though I'm feeling really stressed about [the thing], I accept how I'm feeling right now.

[pause 3s]

Even though my mind is spinning and I don't know how to calm down, I'm okay exactly as I am.

[pause 3s]

Even though this feels so big, I'm choosing to let some of it go now.

[pause 2s]

---

[rhythmic, steady — moderate pace]

Now we're going to tap through each point. I'll guide you. Just tap each spot gently about five to seven times as we go.

[rhythmic]

Eyebrow — All this stress about [the thing]…

Side of eye — Feeling it in my chest, stomach, head…

Under eye — This worry that won't stop…

Under nose — All this pressure…

Chin — I feel so overwhelmed…

Collarbone — This feeling of not being enough…

Under arm — I just want to escape this…

Top of head — All this stress…

[pause 3s]

[encouraging]

Good. Take a deep breath.

[pause 3s]

Let's go again. This time, we're shifting toward acceptance.

Eyebrow — Even with all this stress…

Side of eye — I'm still here, doing my best…

Under eye — I don't have to have it all figured out…

Under nose — It's okay to feel this way…

Chin — I'm not alone in this…

Collarbone — I can get through this…

Under arm — One step at a time…

Top of head — I choose to let go of some of this pressure…

[pause 4s]

[softer]

Great. Check your number. Has it gone down?

[pause 3s]

One more round, focusing on relief.

Eyebrow — This remaining stress…

Side of eye — I'm allowed to feel better…

Under eye — Releasing it now…

Under nose — Letting it go…

Chin — I'm okay…

Collarbone — I can handle what comes…

Under arm — I am more than this stress…

Top of head — Peace. Just peace.

---

[grounding, warm]

Take a deep breath in… and out.

[pause 4s]

Another one. In through your nose, out through your mouth.

[pause 4s]

Check your number again. Notice if anything shifted.

[pause 4s]

Whatever number it is, that's okay. You just did something really smart — you used your body to help your brain calm down.

[pause 3s]

The more you tap, the easier it gets. Your brain starts to learn: "Oh, when I tap, I can relax."

[pause 3s]

[affirming]

Give yourself credit. You showed up. That's what matters.

[pause 2s]

You've got this.

[END]
