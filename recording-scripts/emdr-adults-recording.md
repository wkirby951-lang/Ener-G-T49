## Narration Script

[Deep, unhurried, compassionate — spacious delivery throughout]

Welcome. I want to acknowledge something before we begin: the fact that you're here, choosing to do this work, speaks to your resilience. Healing is not a luxury — it's a necessity, and you deserve it.

[pause 3s]

Take a moment to arrive. Adjust your body so you feel both supported and alert. Let your hands rest wherever they're comfortable. Let your feet be grounded.

[pause 5s]

Let's do a slow breath together. Breathe in through your nose for four counts… 1… 2… 3… 4. Hold it. And out through your mouth for six counts… 1… 2… 3… 4… 5… 6.

[pause 5s]

One more time. In for four. Hold. Out for six.

[pause 6s]

Today, we're going to work with a memory that still carries emotional weight. Something that feels unfinished or unresolved — or simply a recurring stress that drains your energy.

[pause 4s]

Let the memory surface naturally. Don't search for it — let it find you.

[pause 8s]

[pause, soft]

Good. Now, what image represents the most distressing part of that memory? A single frame, like a photograph.

[pause 6s]

What negative belief about yourself is connected to this memory — something like "I failed," "I'm alone," "I'm not safe," "I'm not worthy," "I can't handle this"?

[pause 6s]

What emotions are present as you hold this memory? Name them — grief, anger, fear, shame, numbness, regret, or something else.

[pause 5s]

And where do you feel this in your body right now? Notice any sensations — heaviness, tightness, burning, emptiness, flutter.

[pause 6s]

On a scale from 0 to 10 — 0 being no disturbance at all, 10 being the worst you can imagine — what is your level of disturbance right now?

[pause 5s]

Hold that number without judging it. It's just information.

[pause 4s]

Now, what would you rather believe? What positive cognition would you like to replace the negative one? Something like "I did the best I could," "I am safe now," "I am worthy of peace," "I am resilient," "I am not alone."

[pause 6s]

Let's take one more breath together before we begin the processing.

[pause 4s]

---

[steady, rhythmic — bilateral stimulation begins]

Bring the image to mind. The negative belief. The feelings in your body. You don't need to hold onto them tightly — just let them be present… and follow the light.

[guide through silently — 45 seconds bilateral stimulation]

[gentle, warm]

Take a soft breath.

[pause 5s]

What are you noticing now? A shift? A new memory? A sensation? An emotion that's surfacing? Just notice. Don't analyze. Don't fix.

[pause 10s]

Whatever is there is exactly what needs to be there. Stay with it. Let's go again.

[guide through silently — 50 seconds bilateral stimulation]

Breathe. Let your eyes follow easily. Soft gaze. No effort.

[pause 5s]

Check in — has anything changed? The image, the feeling, the thought?

[pause 8s]

Let's continue. Your brain is doing the work. Trust it.

[guide through silently — 55 seconds bilateral stimulation]

Take a breath. Notice. Allow.

[pause 6s]

You might notice new associations — other memories, insights, body sensations. This is called channeling and it's a sign that processing is happening. Stay with whatever comes.

[guide through silently — 60 seconds bilateral stimulation]

Deep breath in… and out…

[pause 6s]

Check your disturbance level again. Moving? Staying the same? Just observe.

[pause 8s]

Let's continue following where your mind wants to go.

[guide through silently — 60 seconds bilateral stimulation]

Breathe. You are safe here. This is temporary discomfort for lasting relief.

[pause 5s]

[guide through silently — 55 seconds bilateral stimulation]

Take a breath. Notice any shifts in your body. Any release — a sigh, a softening, a tear — these are signs of processing.

[pause 8s]

[slowing]

One more round before we move to installation. Stay present.

[guide through silently — 50 seconds bilateral stimulation]

Good. Take a full, deep breath.

---

[softer pace]

Now, let's bring in that positive cognition we identified. Say it to yourself, from a place of quiet conviction.

[pause 6s]

Hold that positive thought and follow the light…

[guide through silently — 40 seconds slower bilateral stimulation]

On a scale from 0 to 10, how true does that positive belief feel to you right now?

[pause 5s]

Let's continue to strengthen it. As you repeat the positive thought, notice where you feel it in your body — maybe a sense of warmth, expansion, lightness.

[guide through silently — 40 seconds bilateral stimulation]

What's the number now?

[pause 5s]

If it's below 7, let's stay with it. What would need to shift for it to feel more true? Bring that shift to mind…

[guide through silently — 40 seconds bilateral stimulation]

Keep holding that positive belief. Let it settle in.

[guide through silently — 30 seconds bilateral stimulation]

[affirming]

Notice how it feels to hold this belief — even for a moment. That feeling is a resource you can return to.

---

[grounding, spacious]

Take a slow, full breath. Scan your body from the top of your head down to your toes.

[pause 5s]

Crown of your head… your forehead and jaw… are you holding tension there? Soften.

[pause 5s]

Neck and shoulders — let them drop. Your shoulders have been carrying a lot.

[pause 5s]

Chest and heart space — breathe into any tightness there.

[pause 5s]

Stomach and abdomen — soft and full.

[pause 5s]

Hips, legs, feet — feel the support beneath you.

[pause 6s]

If you notice any remaining tension, bring your breath to that area. Imagine it softening, releasing.

[pause 8s]

---

[closing, warm]

We're coming to a close now. This was significant work.

[pause 3s]

Let's ground ourselves in the present. Place a hand on your heart and a hand on your belly.

[pause 4s]

Breathe into your hands. Feel your own warmth. Remind yourself: "I am here. I am safe. This is now."

[pause 6s]

Look around your space. Name three things you see — out loud or in your mind.

[pause 6s]

Three sounds you hear.

[pause 5s]

Move your body gently — roll your shoulders, wiggle your fingers and toes.

[pause 5s]

[deeply sincere]

You've done important work today. Your brain has been processing, integrating, healing. Give yourself credit for that.

[pause 4s]

Over the next hours and days, you may notice dreams, memories, or emotions surfacing. This is normal — it means the processing is continuing. Be gentle with yourself. Hydrate. Rest. Journal if that feels supportive.

[pause 4s]

If anything feels too heavy to carry alone, please reach out. Talk to a trusted friend, a family member, or a licensed therapist. You don't have to heal in isolation.

[pause 3s]

Thank you for trusting yourself and this process. Take one final, full breath together. In… hold… and out.

[pause 4s]

When you're ready, gently open your eyes and return to the room.

[END]
