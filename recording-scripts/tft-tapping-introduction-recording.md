## Narration Script

[Warm, informative, clear]

Thought Field Therapy, or TFT, is the original tapping modality. It was developed by Dr. Roger Callahan in the 1980s. Dr. Callahan discovered that tapping on specific acupuncture meridian endpoints while focusing on a distressing thought could eliminate anxiety — sometimes instantly. TFT is the foundation upon which EFT and Faster EFT were later built.

[pause 2s]

TFT is based on the concept of thought fields — the specific bio-energetic patterns created by disturbing thoughts. According to TFT theory, emotional distress is caused by a disruption in the body's energy system. Tapping on specific points in a prescribed sequence — called an algorithm — rebalances the energy system and removes the distress.

[pause 2s]

A key distinction of TFT is its diagnostic approach. While EFT uses a one-size-fits-all sequence, TFT can use voice technology — where the practitioner listens to subtle changes in the client's voice to determine which meridian points are involved. It also uses pre-tested algorithms for specific problems, and a technique called collarbone breathing to check and repair the energy system.

[pause 2s]

The standard TFT algorithm for general anxiety follows this sequence. First, tap the karate chop point with a setup statement. Then tap under eye, under arm, and collarbone — five taps each. Then the 9-Gamut procedure — tapping the Gamut point on the back of your hand continuously while doing nine specific actions including closing and opening your eyes, eye movements, humming, and counting. Then repeat the three-point sequence. Then re-rate your intensity.

[pause 2s]

The 9-Gamut procedure is unique to TFT. It involves continuous tapping on the Gamut point — the back of the hand between the ring and pinky knuckles — while performing nine brain-stimulating actions. It's designed to balance both brain hemispheres.

[pause 2s]

Who benefits from TFT? People who want a structured, algorithmic approach — less free-form than EFT. Those who respond well to specific recipes for specific problems. And it's effective for anxiety, phobias, anger, grief, trauma, and obsessive thoughts.

[pause 2s]

TFT sessions are more structured than EFT. You follow a precise tapping sequence based on the algorithm for your specific issue. Some find the 9-Gamut procedure unusual, but many report powerful shifts.

This information is for educational purposes. For complex conditions, seek a licensed professional.

[END]
